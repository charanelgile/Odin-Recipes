Pasta Primavera with Cauliflower Sauce

All The Primavera Goodness with a Twist of Cauliflower

This is just the right combination of pasta and vegetable.
If you're not using high-protein pasta, you can double the protein per serving by adding 8 ounces cooked and sliced chicken breast.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 cups cauliflower florets
  • 3/4 cup vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese, plus more for garnish
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 6 ounces multigrain spaghetti, uncooked
  • 4 cups broccoli florets, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-size strips
  • 2 tablespoons sliced fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest (Optional)
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Prepare the Cauliflower Sauce
    2. Heat oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; cook, stirring occasionally, until onion is tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Stir in cauliflower and broth; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer, covered, until tender, about 15 minutes. Uncover and let cool slightly. Transfer to a food processor. Add cheese, water, and black pepper. Cover and pulse until smooth.
  2. Cook the Pasta
    3. Meanwhile, cook pasta according to package directions, adding broccoli and bell pepper the last 5 minutes. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup cooking water.
  3. Mix Together
    4. Return pasta mixture to pot and stir in cauliflower sauce. Heat through, stirring in reserved cooking water (if needed) to reach desired consistency. Garnish with basil, zest (if using), lemon juice, and additional cheese.
